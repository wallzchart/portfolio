🖼️ Project 1: Culture Jam

Artist Statement: My Culture Jam assignment titled "Shot by iPhone" critiques Apple's restricted app ecosystems, closed hardware-software integration, and exclusivity. Apple limits consumer choice, preventing users from freely repairing their devices. This piece represents how their business model forces customers to upgrade, limiting longevity and user autonomy.

📺 Project 2: Video Remix

Artist Statement: My remix piece explores the evolution of human communication—from the simplicity of spoken word to the digital sophistication of the internet. While technology promised to bring people together, I question if it has actually led to more isolation.
